What We're Making

The Shows

Look, there's enough cooking content out there. We're not trying to reinvent anything. We just want to make videos that don't insult your intelligence.

Kitchen Sessions

Full cook-alongs where we walk you through the whole thing. No jump cuts that skip the important parts. If something goes wrong, you'll see how we fix it. That's kind of the point.

Quick Hits

60-second tips that actually help. How to sharpen a knife properly. Why your sauce split. Little things that make a difference when you're cooking at home.
