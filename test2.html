The Show Concept

Crocked 'N Loaded bridges the gap between professional culinary expertise and practical home cooking. Unlike traditional cooking shows that focus on restaurant-style perfection, we focus on techniques and recipes that work in real home kitchens.

Each episode features Steve Mackey applying his 23 years of professional experience to teach restaurant-quality techniques in a way that's accessible to home cooks. Eric Landry provides the perspective of an enthusiastic home cook, asking the questions that viewers would ask and demonstrating how these techniques can be applied in everyday cooking.

Our mission is simple: to take professional culinary knowledge and make it practical, accessible, and enjoyable for anyone who loves to cook at home.
