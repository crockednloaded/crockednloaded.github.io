THE RUCKUS

Crocked 'N Loaded isn't your typical cooking show. We're here to cut through the foodie nonsense. No perfectly plated dishes you'll never make. No pretentious chef talk. Just two guys who genuinely care about good food.

WARNING: ACTUAL COOKING ADVICE INSIDE

We do it all: from home cooking demos where we show you how to make restaurant-quality meals at home, to shopping challenges where we cook amazing meals with the cheapest ingredients we can find, to honest restaurant reviews where we tell you what's actually worth your money.

MACK3Y THE FOODIE brings 23 years of professional chef experience - from diners to Four Diamond restaurants - to teach you techniques that actually work. CrockPotDaddy brings the home cook perspective, asking the questions regular people actually have.

Plus, we're hitting the streets for restaurant reviews and interviews - coming soon to YouTube. No filters. No nonsense. Just real food.
