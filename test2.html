THE SHOW

Crocked 'N Loaded is a cooking show with a different flavor. We're not here to show you impossible restaurant techniques or use ingredients you can't find.

Eric (CrockPotDaddy) represents the home cook - learning, experimenting, and sometimes making mistakes. Steve (MACK3Y) brings 23 years of professional experience to break down techniques so anyone can understand them.

Together, we create a show where professional meets practical. Steve teaches the techniques he's mastered over two decades, while Eric asks the questions a home cook would ask. The result is a cooking show that's actually useful for real people cooking in real kitchens.

No fancy studio kitchen. No impossible recipes. Just two friends cooking good food and showing you how to do it too.
