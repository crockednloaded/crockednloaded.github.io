Meet Your Host

Chef Alex Morgan

Executive Chef & Show Creator

With over 15 years of culinary experience spanning three continents, Chef Alex brings a wealth of knowledge and a passion for innovative cooking to Crocked 'N Loaded.

Formerly the head chef at several Michelin-starred restaurants, Alex decided to bring his expertise to television to share his love for bold flavors and creative cooking techniques with home cooks everywhere.

"Cooking shouldn't be intimidating. It should be an adventure where you're free to experiment and make the recipes your own."
