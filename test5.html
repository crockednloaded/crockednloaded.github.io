About Crocked N' Loaded

Crocked N' Loaded is a culinary YouTube network founded by two passionate food enthusiasts: CrockPotDaddy, the master of comfort food, and MACK3Y the Foodie, the gourmet adventurer.

Our mission is to make exceptional cooking accessible to everyone. Whether you're a beginner looking for easy slow-cooker recipes or an experienced cook wanting to tackle advanced techniques, we've got you covered.

We upload new videos every Tuesday and Friday, with special collaboration episodes on the first Sunday of each month. Join our community of over 250,000 subscribers who are passionate about cooking, eating, and sharing great food.
